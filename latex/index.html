Latex Ipsum Dolor Sit Amet

Apr 26, 2019 • Baskin Senbaslar

\begin{align*} \text{minimize } & \int_{0}^{T_i}\left\|f_i(t)-o_i(t)\right\|^2 dt\\ \text{subject to }& \\ &f_i(t) \text{ is}\text{ continuous up to degree $c$},\\ &\frac{d^jf_i}{dt^j}(0) = \frac{d^jp_i}{dt^j}(0)\text{ for } j\in\{0,1,...,c\}\\ &f_i(t)\text{ is collision-free, and}\\ &\left\|\frac{d^k f_i(t)}{dt^k}\right\| \leq \gamma_k\text{ for all desired $k$},\\ \text{where } & t\in [0,T_i]. \end{align*}
